Dating Profile:

The thematic idea in this section of the project is Beneatha's want for true love. I'm focusing on making a dating profile that I beleive she would like. I am going to be appealing to the fact that she is an intellectual with the want of real love, not money. I chose this highly red pallet for the fact that she wants love not money, therefore its red like a heart.

Beneatha

  • Single!!!!
  • Ready to have genuine conversations with another intellectual...
  • Doctor in training!

I love having deep conversations, helping others and learning new things! I am going to school to be a doctor because my dream is to help others. It's intellectually stimulating and I think if you're also going to school we may be compatible. I'm not... traditional, I will not let anyone tell me what I can and can't do, and that includes you, I am going to be what I want regardless of what you think of it. Not to mention if I don't feel a connection to you I will NOT stay! No ammount of money can keep me in your arms, if we don't connect, its over.
